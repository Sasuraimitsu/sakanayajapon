Deadline & Delivery

Q: Is there an order deadline?A: Orders must be placed by 4:30 PM, at least 2 hours before your desired delivery time.
Q: Can I place a same-day order?A: Yes, same-day orders are accepted.
Q: When will my order arrive?A: Please check the ready time in the LINE order system at checkout.

Q: How is my order delivered?A: We use GRAB EXPRESS for delivery.
Q: What is the delivery fee?A: Delivery starts from Central Market via GRAB EXPRESS. Customers are responsible for delivery fees.

Payment

Q: What payment methods do you accept?A: We accept payment via QR code.
Q: Can I pay in US dollars?A: Yes, although your account is in riel, you may also pay in US dollars.
Q: What if the product arrives in poor condition?A: Please contact us immediately upon receipt. We will assess the situation and respond accordingly.
